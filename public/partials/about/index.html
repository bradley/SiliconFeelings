Likely Questions

Q. What is EmojiEarth?

A. EmojiEarth is a real time display of Emoji being tweeted by people across Earth. The application reads Tweets coming from Twitter, extracts any Emoji, and ignores those that have not been tagged as coming from any particular location.

Q. Does EmojiEarth display every Emoji tweeted?

A. No. Twitter limits this application to tracking a maximum of 400 out of the roughly 800 Emoji. I used emojitracker.com to determine the top 400 most frequently tweeted Emoji in order to make this less unpleasant.

Q. I tried tweeting with Emoji and it didn't show up…

A. This application can only display Emoji from Tweets sent to it by Twitter. It can’t be guaranteed that the application will be sent every Tweet containing Emoji at all times. Remember to ensure you have allowed your tweets to include location data.
