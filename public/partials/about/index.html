Questions and Answers

Q. What is Silicon Feelings?

A. Silicon Feelings is a real time display of Emoji being tweeted by people across Earth.

Q. Does Silicon Feelings display every Emoji tweeted?

A. No. First, the application ignores any Tweets that have not been tagged as coming from any particular location. Twitter also limits this application to tracking a maximum of 400 out of the roughly 800 Emoji. I use emojitracker.com to determine the top 400 most frequently tweeted Emoji in order to make this less unpleasant.

Q. I tried tweeting with Emoji and it didn't show up…

A. This application can only display Emoji from Tweets sent to it by Twitter. It can’t be guaranteed that the application will be sent every Tweet containing Emoji at all times. Remember to ensure you have allowed your tweets to include location data.
